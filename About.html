OUR STORY


Emrich Property Consults

We are an independent Professional firm specialising in Property Consultancy Services including; Asset Valuation, Cost Estimation, Property Research, Agency, Land Survey and Boundary Verification and Credit Advisory Services with considerable experience in handling a wide range of large and small projects for government, parastatal, commercial and private clients in the region. The company includes valuers, surveyors, engineers, technicians, support staff and specialist consultants including architects, all able to offer comprehensive services from the initial feasibility study through to the completed contract on all types of work. Alternatively, the scope of the service may be tailored to suite a client’s specific needs.
